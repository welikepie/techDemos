We Like Pie Demos

This is the playground of We Like Pie. Here you can find tools we have built and experiments we are working on. Try them out for yourself!

  • Be The Buyer

    A google hangout created for TopShop to promote both their newest collections and the London Fashion Week. The user can watch models on the runway and add the clothes shown to their own personal collections, which they can then share with their friends.
